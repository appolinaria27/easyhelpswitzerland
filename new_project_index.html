Who we are

I am Polina Kravtsova. I help people moving to Switzerland or already building their life here with permits, registrations, legal communication and practical relocation support.

My own path included study, residence procedures and building a family life in Switzerland. I arrived without language and without a clear system around me, so I know how confusing these steps can feel in real life.

Together with Swiss legal education and work in a migration-focused law firm, this experience allows me to offer support that is practical, clear and accessible.

Whether you are coming for work, study, marriage, family life or simply trying to improve your administrative position in Switzerland, I focus on helping you take the next correct step.

My mission is simple: reduce stress, create structure, and help you move forward without unnecessary delays or legal costs.

My goal is to help every client find a clear route in Switzerland with ease, preparation and confidence.
