Who supports you in Switzerland?

Polina Kravtsova

Relocation Consultant · Zürich

My name is Polina Kravtsova. I support international clients with relocation, permits, and administrative processes in Switzerland. My goal is simple — make complex procedures clear, structured, and manageable. I am studying for a Master's degree at the University of Zurich, with a focus on migration and administrative law.

I know how hard it can be to move to a new country — I started from zero myself. Now I want to help others save time and avoid confusion when dealing with the Swiss system. My experience as a legal assistant at a law firm in Zürich helps me find practical, realistic paths for each client. Instead of uncertainty, you get clear steps and personal support.
